PomoPlan - Data Deletion Request

PomoPlan does not maintain any server-side user accounts. All your data (tasks, habits, sessions, settings) is stored locally on your device.

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1 – Delete all local data

  1. Go to your device's Settings → Apps → PomoPlan
  2. Tap Clear Data
  3. All local data will be permanently deleted

Option 2 – Uninstall the app

Uninstalling PomoPlan removes all locally stored data from your device.

Cloud Backup Data

If you used the cloud backup feature, your backup is stored in your personal Google Drive under the folder PomoPlan. To delete it:

  1. Open Google Drive
  2. Find and delete the PomoPlan folder
