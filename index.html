PomoPlan Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

PomoPlan ("we", "our", or "the app") is a productivity app developed by OdakStudio. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

PomoPlan stores all tasks, habits, Pomodoro sessions, and settings locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit this data to our servers.

Cloud Backup

If you choose to use the cloud backup feature, your data is saved to your personal Google Drive account. We do not have access to your Google Drive or the backed-up data. This feature requires Google Sign-In.

Advertising

The free version of PomoPlan displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use your advertising ID and device information to serve personalized ads. You can learn more at Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions and one-time purchases are processed through Google Play and managed by RevenueCat. We do not store your payment information. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Permissions

  • Notifications: Used for Pomodoro timer alerts and daily reminders.
  • Foreground Service: Used to keep the timer running when the app is in the background.
  • Boot Completed: Used to restore scheduled reminders after device restart.

Children's Privacy

PomoPlan is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at info.pomoplan@gmail.com.
